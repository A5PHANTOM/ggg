WOMEN'S-DIET PLAN

PREMIUM AVAILABLE

To maintain a good body structure and overall health, women can focus on a combination of balanced nutrition, exercise, and healthy habits.

Customizing for Your Needs

For Bulking: Add an extra serving of carbs (e.g., rice or roti) and increase the portion size of protein-rich foods like paneer or tofu.

For Maintenance/Lean Muscle: Keep portions moderate and focus on whole grains and fiber-rich vegetables.

Key Principles for Nutrition

  • 1. Protein: Essential for muscle repair and growth.
    Sources: Eggs, chicken, fish, turkey, lean mutton, prawns.
  • 2. Carbohydrates: For energy and recovery.
    Sources: Whole grains (brown rice, quinoa, roti), sweet potatoes, oats, fruits.
  • 3. Healthy Fats: For hormonal health and energy.
    Sources: Nuts, seeds, ghee, coconut, avocado.
  • 4. Vegetables: For vitamins, minerals, and fiber.
    Include greens, root vegetables, and colorful options.
  • 5. Hydration: Drink plenty of water and include electrolyte-rich drinks like coconut water.

Starches:

Old-fashioned oats • Quinoa • Sweet potatoes • Russet potatoes • Brown rice • Whole wheat pasta • Whole wheat bread • Chickpeas • Black beans • Butternut squash • Millet

Proteins:

Large eggs • Liquid egg whites • Greek yogurt (full-fat) • Cottage cheese (2% or full-fat) • Chicken breasts • Lean ground turkey • Salmon fillets • Tofu • Tempeh • Canned tuna • Boneless skinless chicken thighs • Edamame • Ground chicken or turkey

Fruits and Vegetables:

Bananas • Blueberries • Strawberries • Apples • Spinach • Kale • Fresh cauliflower • Broccoli • Zucchini • Sweet bell peppers • Baby carrots • Avocados • Cucumbers • Cherry tomatoes • Beets • Pears • Cantaloupe • Watermelon

Other Items:

Olive oil • Coconut oil • Almond milk (unsweetened) • Almond flour • Natural peanut butter or almond butter • Chia seeds • Flaxseeds • Whey protein powder • Casein protein powder • Low-sodium vegetable broth • Turmeric • Garlic • Stevia or honey • Dark chocolate (70% cocoa or higher) • Green tea

SUGGESTED RECIPES

Breakfast

  • 2 boiled eggs or an omelet with mixed veggies + 1 or 2 multigrain roti or avocado toast.
  • Oats smoothie:
    • 1 cup oats and milk, with added almonds, walnuts, dates, blueberries, and a banana.
    • Add a spoonful of honey or peanut butter for extra calories.
    • Add whey protein (optional).
  • 4-5 egg whites
  • Sweet potato (100 g)
  • Chana chaat (boiled chickpeas, tomatoes, onions, cucumber, lemon, and spices).
  • A small piece of fruit (mango, papaya, or apple).

Lunch

  • Brown rice (100g) + Chicken breast (250g)
  • Cucumber (1 medium)
  • Fish curry or grilled fish (250g) (made with minimal oil, with cream and Indian spices)
  • Brown rice (100g)

    DINNER

  • Grilled chicken breast (100g)
  • Sautéed spinach or cabbage with mustard seeds and a bit of turmeric.
  • Whole wheat chapathi (1-2)
  • Chicken tikka (grilled 100g) or baked chicken with Indian spices.
  • Vegetable stir fry (use minimal oil, with garlic, cumin, and coriander for flavor).
  • Brown rice (100g)
  • Prawns curry (100g)
  • Sautéed spinach or cabbage with mustard seeds and a bit of turmeric.
  • Whole wheat chapathi (1-2) or rice (100g)

Snacks

  • 2 slices of bread with peanut butter and 1 banana
  • Greek yogurt (200g) with mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts) and a handful of raisins or dates
  • Boiled sweet potato (150g) with a dash of cinnamon
  • Hummus (3 tbsp) with carrot and cucumber sticks

    Before Bed

  • Cottage cheese (100g) with a pinch of black pepper or chili powder.
  • A handful of mixed nuts or pumpkin seeds.
  • Greek yogurt (100-150g) with honey (1 tsp) or fruit (berries or apple slices).
  • A boiled egg.
  • Milk (1 glass) with almonds or cashews (5-6 pieces).

ADDITIONAL TIPS

  • Supplements: Consider supplementing with:
    • Whey protein (to meet protein goals)
    • Creatine (to support strength and muscle growth)
    • BCAA (for recovery)
    • Fish oil (for healthy fats and joint support)
  • Caloric Tracking: Track your calories if possible, to ensure you're in a surplus without gaining unnecessary fat.
  • Cooking Methods: Opt for grilling, baking, steaming, and stir-frying with minimal oil.
